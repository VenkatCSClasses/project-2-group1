Meeting notes Apr 8:
* Routes are collected by files, related routes go in the same file.
    * Routes are collected in main.ts to a certain scope. When in accounts, you define GET / or POST /create, but then main.ts imports these scoped as GET /api/accounts/ or POST /api/accounts/create
* What happens when a manager leaves?
    * Must have one other manager to leave or it will error
    * Can promote other manager through existing UI
    * Last member that leaves → delete household
* HTMX - adds get/post/etc requests to HTML attributes
    * Examples in repo/documentation in NotebookLM chat context if people have questions, or ask Sam on slack/via email
